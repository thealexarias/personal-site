My Backpacking System

2025-12-23

This is for the folks who want to travel to a different country with just a backpack. No checked luggage and not too many possessions.

This is the gear I use and the system I've built around it. I believe this system can work for generally any male above the age of 14. I won't pretend to know what a woman might need while backpacking a different country.

Quick credential check. I've backpacked through parts of Europe and Japan for 2+ months each. Prior to these expeditions, I was in the Navy and lived on a ship, so naturally, I was living with minimal possessions here, too.
